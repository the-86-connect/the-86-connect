About 86 Connect

Our Story
Founded 2015

A Decade of Bridging China & the World

86 Connect was founded with a clear mission: to be the most trusted gateway between China and the world. The number 86represents China's international dialing code, symbolizing our role as the direct connection to everything China has to offer.

What started as a small consultancy helping international students navigate Chinese universities has grown into a comprehensive service platform supporting both education and business opportunities across China.

Our Mission

Simplifying Access to China's Opportunities

We exist to simplify access to China's educational and economic opportunities. Whether you're a student seeking a world-class education or a business looking to source quality products, we provide the expertise, connections, and support you need to succeed.

Our team combines deep local knowledge with international experience, ensuring you receive culturally informed, practically useful guidance at every step.
